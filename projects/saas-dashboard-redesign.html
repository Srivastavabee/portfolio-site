SaaS Dashboard Redesign

SaaS • Analytics • UX

Overview

Problem: Cluttered dashboard → low adoption, early churn within first 14 days.

Role: Product Manager (Freelance)

Timeline: 8 weeks

Team: PM, 1 Designer, 2 Engineers

Empathy / Research

  • ~20 user interviews and 2 surveys.
  • Users lost in deep nav and dense widgets.

Define

  • Improve first-14-day activation & reduce onboarding drop-off.
  • KPIs: D7 retention, 14-day drop-off.

Ideate

  • Role-based modular widgets.
  • Simplified IA and navigation.

Prototype

  • Low-fi → high-fi Figma; PRDs in Notion.
  • Two sprints with backlog grooming.

Metrics & Impact

  • Retention (D7): 100 → 125 (+25%)
  • Drop-off (lower): 100 → 88 (−12%)

What I’d Improve Next

  • Role-based personalization.
  • Contextual onboarding checklist.
  • Widget marketplace for power users.

Note: Metrics are indexed (Pre=100) for confidentiality.
