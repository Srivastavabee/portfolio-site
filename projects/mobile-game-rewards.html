Mobile Game Rewards System

Gaming • Engagement • Monetization

Overview

Problem: Good installs, weak engagement and short sessions.

Role: Product Manager (Freelance)

Timeline: 6 weeks

Team: PM, 1 Designer, 2 Engineers

Empathy / Research

  • Analyzed play patterns; big drop after day 3.
  • Community feedback + ratings.

Define

  • Increase daily return rate & session length.
  • Hypothesis: habit-forming rewards increase stickiness.

Ideate

  • Daily login bonuses, milestone rewards, unlockables.
  • Session goals with gentle nudges.

Prototype

  • Progression loop in Figma.
  • A/B test with ~2,000 users.

Metrics & Impact

  • Session Length: 100 → 120 (+20%)
  • DAU: 100 → 117 (+17%)

What I’d Improve Next

  • Seasonal events & time-limited quests.
  • Economy balancing.
  • Personalized offers by cohort.

Note: Metrics are indexed (Pre=100) for confidentiality.
