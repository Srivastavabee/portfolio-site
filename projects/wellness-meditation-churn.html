Wellness/Meditation App – Churn Reduction

Wellness • Retention • Onboarding

Overview

Problem: Drop after week 1; confusing onboarding and weak habit formation.

Role: Product Manager (Freelance)

Timeline: 6 weeks

Team: PM, 1 Designer, 2 Engineers

Empathy / Research

  • Cohort analysis (Firebase/Mixpanel) D1→D7 fall-off.
  • Interviews with early-churn users.

Define

  • Increase D7 retention; reduce first-week drop-off.
  • Hypothesis: tooltips + streaks build habit.

Ideate

  • Checklist onboarding, contextual tooltips, reminders.
  • Gamified streak tracker.

Prototype

  • Shipped redesigned onboarding & basic streaks.

Metrics & Impact

  • Retention (D7): 100 → 125 (+25%)
  • Drop-off (lower): 100 → 85 (−15%)

What I’d Improve Next

  • Adaptive nudges by activity.
  • Social/buddy challenges.
  • Content recommendations by engagement.

Note: Metrics are indexed (Pre=100) for confidentiality.
